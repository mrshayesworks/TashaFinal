Why Us?

Crafty Times Childcare is a place where children may learn and grow through creative curriculum design. We pride ourselves on providing a safe, caring atmosphere where we may partner with parents in order to foster academic growth allowing children to become great achievers. We provide a warm, caring, secure environment for children to learn at their own pace. Curriculums are designed based on age groups. Cleanliness is very important and we take every precaution to prevent the spread of germs through disinfecting regularly. Nutrition is also important here at Crafty Times Childcare. We provide breakfast, lunch, and afternoon snacks that satisfy daily nutritional needs and meet licensing requirements. Crafty Times Childcare accepts children from the ages of 6 weeks to 6 years.
